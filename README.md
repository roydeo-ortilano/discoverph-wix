# Discover Philippines – Wix Website Project

**Project Name:** Discover Philippines  
**Platform:** Wix  
**Website URL:** [https://roydeoortilano.wixsite.com/discoverph](https://roydeoortilano.wixsite.com/discoverph)  

## Project Overview
This is a static travel website created as part of a beginner web design training module.  
It showcases the Philippines’ cultural history, islands, and outdoor adventures across Luzon, Visayas, and Mindanao.  
The project demonstrates proficiency in multi-page content integration and professional UI/UX design using Wix.

---

## Objectives
- Create a visually appealing and informative travel website using Wix.  
- Integrate multi-page content effectively.  
- Demonstrate the use of beginner-friendly professional UI/UX principles.  
- Provide engaging content about the Philippines for prospective travelers.

---

## Website Structure

### Pages
- **Home**: Overview of the Philippines, key attractions, islands, and beaches, introduction to cultural quirks and outdoor adventures.  
- **About PH**: In-depth information about the country’s regions and major cities, personal travel reflections, tips for traveling during the monsoon season.  

---

## Content Included

### Introducing Philippines
- Overview of landscapes, cities, wildlife, and the friendly Filipino people.

### Islands & Beaches
- Description of over 7,000 islands, beaches in Visayas, Luzon, Mindanao, and adventure opportunities.

### Why I Love the Philippines (By Greg Bloom)
- Personal experiences highlighting Filipino culture, local food, jeepney rides, karaoke, and unique experiences.

### Tempestuous Tropics
- Advice on climate, monsoon season, and travel flexibility.

### Cultural Quirks
- Highlights Spanish and American colonial influences, Catholic traditions, and Filipino pride.

### Outdoor Adventures
- Activities including surfing, diving, kayaking, trekking, canyoning, and ziplining.

### Places in the Philippines
- **Eastern Visayas** – Resilient region with natural beauty.  
- **Manila** – ‘Pearl of the Orient’, a chaotic yet vibrant megacity.  
- **Western Visayas & Panay** – Boracay, Aklan Province, and cultural festivals.  
- **North Luzon** – Mountains, tribal cultures, surfing, and trekking.  
- **Cebu & Cebu City** – Hub of the Visayas, cultural center, urban and island experiences.  
- **Mindanao & Sulu** – Rugged landscapes, beaches, and indigenous cultures.  
- **Palawan** – Scenic seascapes, islands, and wildlife.  
- **Negros** – Mountains, beaches, diving, and eco-tourism.  
- **The Cordillera** – Mountainous northern Luzon, cultural heritage.  
- **Boracay** – Famous tourist destination for beaches and nightlife.  
- **Northern Mindanao** – Coastal region with outdoor adventure opportunities.  
- **Bohol** – Chocolate Hills, tarsiers, diving on Panglao Island.  
- **Mindoro** – Mountain ranges, rugged western and prosperous eastern provinces.
